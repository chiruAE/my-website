About Me

Doctoral Student, Flow Physics Lab, Mechanical Engineering at Indian Institute of Science (IISc). My area of research in flow past the elastically mounted cylinder and this area extends to investigations of flow-induced vibrations utilizing laser-based techniques for flow visualization. Specifically, I have explored the behavior of elastically mounted cylinders with splitter plates, threads, bubbles on amplitude response, and forces through the use of sensors.
